CASCADE BIOFUELS PRIVATE LIMITED

"Harmonizing Renewable Efforts for Global Sustainability"

MISSION

  • Our Mission is to Revolutionize Plastic Waste Management through advanced technologies and sustainable practices, transforming waste into valuable resources for a cleaner, greener future through advanced pyrolysis technology.This technology offers an environmentally responsible method to transform mixed plastic waste and waste tires into high-quality products, including fuel oil, biodiesel, carbon black, etc thereby enhancing resource efficiency and reducing landfill waste.

Services

Featured Srvices

Plastic waste management

collect plastic waste and convert it to fuel oil through pyrolysis. The final product of the pyrolysis plant is mainly fuel oil (can be further refined into diesel) and uncondensed gas.

Tyre waste Management

collect and convert tyre wastes into fuel oil through pyrolysis. The final product of the pyrolysis plant is mainly fuel oil,carbon black and uncondensed gas
